Description

Mashed potatoes are a classic comfort food made by mashing boiled potatoes and blending them with butter, milk, and seasoning. The potatoes are cooked until tender, then whipped to a smooth consistency, creating a dish that is both hearty and satisfying for any occasion.

This creamy and smooth dish is perfect as a side for many meals, offering a rich flavor and satisfying texture. Whether served alongside roasted meats or enjoyed on its own, mashed potatoes bring warmth and comfort to the table, making them a favorite in households around the world.

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cream Cheese
  • Toppings (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes.
  2. Boil the potatoes.
  3. Prepare your melted butter mixture.
  4. Pan-dry the potatoes.
  5. Mash the potatoes.
  6. Stir everything together.
  7. Taste and season.
  8. Serve warm.
